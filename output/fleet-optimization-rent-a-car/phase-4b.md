## Individual Study Results
*(PRISMA Item 19)*

### Reporting Approach

Because no meta-analysis is planned for this review (see Phase 3c, Item 13d), summary statistics per group (N, mean, SD for continuous outcomes; events/total for binary outcomes) are not required in the meta-analytic sense. Instead, Item 19 is operationalized as an individual-study outcome extraction table — one row per study-outcome combination — presenting raw extracted values in the units reported by each study, alongside the derived effect measure where computable.

Where a study was identified across multiple reports (e.g., a conference paper followed by a full journal article reporting the same dataset), the primary report providing the most complete outcome data is used as the source record and all supplementary reports are cross-referenced in the Notes column.
