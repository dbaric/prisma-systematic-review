# Phase 1: Title & Abstract — PRISMA Items 1–12

## Dependencies
> None — this is the first phase.

## Steps

1. **Item 1: Title Identification**
   - Identify the report as a systematic review
   - State the population, intervention, comparator, and outcome (PICO format)
   - If updating a previous review, identify this in the title and cite the original review
   → Write to: `output/{theme}/phase-1.md` → `## Title`

2. **Items 2–12: Structured Abstract**
   Create a structured abstract containing all 12 PRISMA 2020 abstract items:
   
   - **Item 2: Background/Rationale** — One sentence on why the review was needed and the evidence gap being addressed
   - **Item 3: Objectives** — Research question using explicit PICO components (Population, Intervention, Comparator, Outcome); include time horizon if relevant
   - **Item 4: Eligibility Criteria** — Study designs, population, key inclusion/exclusion criteria (minimum 2–3 criteria)
   - **Item 5: Information Sources** — All databases and registries searched; provide search date(s)
   - **Item 6: Risk of Bias Methods** — Tool(s) used to assess risk of bias at the study level
   - **Item 7: Synthesis Methods** — Whether meta-analysis or narrative synthesis was used; if meta-analysis, name the statistical model
   - **Item 8: Study Selection and Counts** — `[DATA REQUIRED: number screened, assessed for full-text eligibility, and included]` — Do not fabricate these numbers.
   - **Item 9: Synthesis Results** — `[DATA REQUIRED: primary summary estimate with 95% CI and I² heterogeneity measure; or narrative direction and consistency]` — Do not fabricate effect sizes or statistics.
   - **Item 10: Limitations** — 1–2 sentences on main limitations of evidence base and review process
   - **Item 11: Conclusions** — Main finding and direct implication for clinical practice, policy, or further research
   - **Item 12: Funding and Registration** — Name funders (or "No funding"); register name and full registration number (or "Not registered")
   
   → Write to: `output/{theme}/phase-1.md` → `## Abstract` with each item as a labeled subsection

---

All 12 abstract items must be labeled and explicitly addressed — do not omit or merge items. For updated/living review requirements, see `phases/output-format.md` → Special Requirements.
