# Phase 2: Introduction — PRISMA Items 13–14

## Dependencies
> Read `output/{theme}/phase-1.md` → `## Title` to understand your PICO framing before writing this section.

## Steps

1. **Item 13: Rationale**
   - Describe current state of knowledge
   - Explain why the review is necessary
   - Articulate the gap being addressed
   
   **Guidance:** Write 2–3 paragraphs (~200–250 words) grounded in the stated PICO (population and identified gap). Do not cite specific studies unless provided in the research input. Do not inject generic claims from pre-training; stay focused on the question at hand.
   → Write to: `output/{theme}/phase-2.md` → `## Rationale`

2. **Item 14: Objectives**
   - State the specific research question(s) in PICO format
   - If updating a previous review:
     - Cite the original review
     - Specify which aspects are being updated (scope, methods, evidence sources, outcomes)
     - Explain why the update is necessary
   → Write to: `output/{theme}/phase-2.md` → `## Objectives`
